Frequently Asked Questions

When will Sentimark be available?

Sentimark is currently in development, with our Kickstarter campaign scheduled to launch in Summer 2025. Early access for backers will begin in Fall 2025, with full platform launch in Winter 2025.

How can I support the Kickstarter campaign?

You can sign up for our newsletter to be notified when the campaign launches. Once live, you'll be able to back the project at various tiers to receive early access and special benefits.

Do you offer partnerships or affiliate programs?

We're exploring partnership opportunities with financial platforms, content providers, and educational institutions. For partnership inquiries, please use our contact form and select "Partnership Opportunity" as the subject.

Are you hiring?

While we don't currently have open positions, we're always interested in connecting with talented individuals passionate about AI and financial markets. Please use our contact form with "General Inquiry" as the subject and include details about your experience.

Have a question not answered here? Please contact us or check our Kickstarter FAQ for more information.
