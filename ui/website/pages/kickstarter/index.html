Frequently Asked Questions

When will the Kickstarter campaign launch?

+

Our Kickstarter campaign is scheduled to launch in Summer 2025. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when the campaign goes live.

Get Notified

What happens if the campaign doesn't reach its funding goal?

+

Sentimark is committed to launching regardless of the Kickstarter outcome. If we don't reach our goal, we have alternative funding strategies in place, though early access and special benefits may be more limited.

When will backers receive access to the platform?

+

Early access beta is scheduled for Fall 2025, with the full platform launch in Winter 2025. Specific access timing will depend on your backer tier:

  • Professional tier: First access in early Fall 2025
  • Sentiment Explorer tier: Access in mid-Fall 2025
  • Early Bird tier: Access in late Fall 2025

Can I upgrade my backer tier after the campaign?

+

Yes, backers will have the opportunity to upgrade their tier after the campaign through our backer portal. However, certain limited-availability tiers may no longer be available.

What platforms will Sentimark be available on?

+

Sentimark will be available as a web application accessible from any modern browser, as well as native mobile apps for iOS and Android devices.

Will there be ongoing costs after my backer period ends?

+

After your included access period ends, you'll have the option to subscribe to Sentimark at the regular price. As a backer, you'll receive a discount on subscription fees based on your backer tier:

  • Professional tier: 30% lifetime discount
  • Sentiment Explorer tier: 25% lifetime discount
  • Early Bird tier: 15% discount for the first year

What data sources will Sentimark analyze?

+

Sentimark analyzes sentiment from a wide range of financial data sources, including:

  • Financial news articles and press releases
  • Earnings call transcripts
  • SEC filings and regulatory documents
  • Social media and financial forums
  • Analyst reports and market commentary

Premium data sources will be available based on subscription tier.

How will backer feedback be incorporated?

+

Backer feedback is critical to our development process. We'll incorporate it through:

  • Regular feedback surveys for all backers
  • Private beta feedback channels
  • Feature prioritization voting for higher tier backers
  • Direct access to our development team during scheduled sessions
