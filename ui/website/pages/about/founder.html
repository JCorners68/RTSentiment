Founded on Deep Technical Expertise

Sentimark was founded by Jonathan, bringing extensive experience across artificial intelligence, financial markets, and enterprise software development to tackle the challenge of uncovering hidden alpha.

Leveraging over 15 years of experience, including leading AI implementations for Fortune 500 companies and financial institutions, I've built Sentimark's core technology and strategy from the ground up. My multidisciplinary background allows for a comprehensive approach to sentiment analysis, creating a uniquely powerful solution.

My passion lies in using advanced technology to reveal hidden market insights. The remarkable progress of Sentimark demonstrates the solid foundation that's been built, with a vision for strategically expanding the team post-funding to accelerate growth and innovation.

Meet the Founder

Jonathan

Founder & CEO

Jonathan brings over 15 years of deep expertise in Artificial Intelligence and Quantitative Finance. With a background encompassing leadership roles in designing enterprise-scale AI systems at a market-leading management consulting firm, he possesses a unique blend of technical leadership and market understanding. His specializations include natural language processing, sentiment analysis technologies, and financial market dynamics.

At Sentimark, Jonathan spearheads technological development, product strategy, and overall vision. He has personally architected the company's agentic AI system, drawing on specialized knowledge in NLP and experience leading complex AI implementations for financial institutions. This comprehensive skillset has enabled the rapid development of Sentimark's core platform, which is now poised to disrupt how sentiment analysis is applied to financial markets.

Jonathan is passionate about democratizing access to sophisticated financial tools, believing that advanced sentiment analysis should be available to investors of all types, not just elite institutions with enterprise budgets.
